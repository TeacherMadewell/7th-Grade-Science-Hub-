Check before the sim

Two NORTH poles brought together will…

✓ push apart — Like poles repel — N and N face off and shove away from each other, just as two positive charges push apart.

Will a magnet stick to an aluminum foil ball?

✓ No — aluminum is not attracted to magnets — Magnets attract iron and steel. Aluminum is a metal, but it has no iron — so the magnet shrugs right past it. 'All metals are magnetic' is the classic wrong idea; only iron and steel answer the call.

Magnetic force requires the magnet to physically touch the object before it can pull.

✓ false — Magnetic force is a noncontact force — it acts across empty space, exactly like electric force. You can feel the pull before the magnet ever touches the steel.
