Lab safety check

Goggles go on before anything is poured or heated.

✓ true — Eye protection goes on before the first pour or flame — not after.

If you spill something, the FIRST thing you do is…

✓ tell Ms. Madewell right away — Stop and tell the teacher immediately — never clean an unknown spill alone.

Long hair near a flame should be…

✓ tied back — Tie hair back and remove loose clothing near any flame.

It's fine to taste a chemical if it looks like food.

✓ false — Never taste anything in the lab. Waft to smell only when told.

Where do backpacks go during a lab?

✓ under the table, aisles clear — Bags under the table keep aisles clear so no one trips during a lab.
