Check before the sim

The soot specks in the smokestack are given a negative charge by the wire. The nearby plate is positive. The force on each speck is…

✓ a pull toward the plate — Opposite charges attract — the negative soot and positive plate pull each other, no contact needed.

Engineers crank the wire voltage way up, putting MORE charge on each soot speck. The pull toward the plate gets…

✓ stronger — More charge turns Dial 1 up — a bigger charge means a bigger electric force, so the pull to the plate increases.

A second smokestack is built with the collector plate twice as far from the wire. All else equal, the force pulling soot to that plate is…

✓ weaker than the first stack — More distance turns Dial 2 the wrong way — spreading charges apart makes the force fade, so the farther plate pulls less.
